FAQ
How do pricing and revenue share work?

We offer multiple ways to start. Revenue sharing is negotiable, and we’ll propose a simple model that fits your property goals.

What’s a typical timeline?

Site walkthrough can happen quickly. After that, installation timing depends on permitting and electrical scope. We keep you on one clear schedule.

We do. Weekly on‑site care keeps the hardware spotless, and support is available when drivers need help.

Can you work with our electrician?

Yes. We can partner with your existing electrician or bring one of ours. Either way, you get a smooth, coordinated install.
